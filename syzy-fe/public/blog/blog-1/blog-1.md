## The Problem with Prediction Markets Today

Most prediction markets today are built on full transparency.

While transparency is a core value of blockchain, in prediction markets it creates a real downside:

- Strategies can be tracked
- Wallet behavior can be analyzed
- Alpha is quickly exposed and copied

In short, users lose their edge the moment they participate.

This is where the demand for **privacy** naturally appears.

## Our Concept: Predict Invisibly

**Oyrade is built around a simple idea:**

**Predictions should be private. Results should be verifiable.**

Oyrade is a privacy-first prediction platform designed to let users participate in markets without exposing their strategies or on-chain behavior.

Instead of exposing every action on-chain, we design Oyrade so that:

- Prediction activity stays private
- User behavior cannot be easily traced
- Ownership and verification are still preserved

Privacy is not an add-on for us : it’s the core design principle.
