# Highlight Features

## 1. Broad Market Coverage

Oyrade supports a diverse range of prediction categories, including digital asset price movements, macroeconomic indicators, ecosystem developments, and narrative-driven events. This multi-sector structure provides participants with access to varied opportunity sets within a unified framework.

## 2. Privacy-Centric Architecture

Confidentiality is embedded at the protocol level. Oyrade integrates **Shielded Trading** architecture powered by Zero-Knowledge Proof technology to minimize traceability between user identities and market positions. Session-based wallet abstraction further separates trading activity from primary wallet exposure, reinforcing strategic discretion and on-chain privacy.

## 3. Streamlined Access and Advantageous Participation

Built on the Solana blockchain, the platform is designed for operational clarity and minimal onboarding friction. You can use a wide range of supported cryptocurrencies to place predictions while benefiting from low transaction fees and fast finality enabled by Solana.

## 4. Automated Liquidity Infrastructure

Oyrade integrates an automated market-making mechanism to ensure continuous liquidity across prediction markets. This structure supports dynamic pricing, efficient execution, and reduced reliance on traditional order book systems.

## 5. Fast Position Execution

You can participate by selecting a direction and quickly buying or selling shares for your chosen position. The process is simple, efficient, and designed for seamless on-chain settlement.
