# Brand Assets

The following Oyrade Brand Assets are protected intellectual property and may only be used in accordance with the guidelines below:

- The Oyrade logo, logotype, and name
- Any official visual materials, including illustrations, interface graphics, and branded design elements

## Permitted Use

Use of Oyrade's Brand Assets is permitted under the following circumstances:

**Product Reference** — You may reference Oyrade's products or services in a factual and accurate manner (e.g., "Place prediction positions on Oyrade").

**Linking** — You may link directly to Oyrade's official website or designated product pages.

**Approved Partnerships** — You may announce an official partnership with Oyrade, provided prior authorization has been granted by an authorized Oyrade representative.

:::warning
Any other use of Oyrade's Brand Assets requires prior written approval from the Oyrade team before publication or distribution.
:::
