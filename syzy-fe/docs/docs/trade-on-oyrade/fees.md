# Fees

Oyrade applies a small trading fee on each prediction. These fees support protocol sustainability, token holder incentives, and long-term ecosystem growth.

## Trading Fees

Oyrade distinguishes between **Public** and **Shielded** predictions.

| Prediction Type | Fee Rate |
|----------------|----------|
| Public | 0.20% |
| Shielded | 0.30% |

- The additional **0.10%** on Shielded predictions supports privacy infrastructure, including encrypted settlement and confidential processing.
- Fees are calculated based on total trade value.

For example, if you place a $100 public prediction, the fee is $0.20. For a $100 shielded prediction, the fee is $0.30.

## Where Fees Go

Trading fee revenue is distributed across three allocations:

| Allocation | Percentage | Purpose |
|------------|-----------|---------|
| Stakers | 30% | Distributed weekly in USDC to users who stake $OYRADE |
| Buyback & Burn | 12% | Used to purchase $OYRADE from the open market and remove it from circulation |
| Operations | 58% | Funds development, infrastructure, and growth initiatives |

This structure aligns user activity with long-term protocol sustainability.

## Fee Discounts

Staking $OYRADE qualifies you for fee reductions based on your tier:

| Tier | Min Stake | Fee Discount | Effective Fee |
|------|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| None | 0 OYRADE | 0% | 0.20% |
| Bronze | 1,000 OYRADE | 10% | 0.18% |
| Silver | 10,000 OYRADE | 20% | 0.16% |
| Gold | 50,000 OYRADE | 30% | 0.14% |
| Diamond | 100,000 OYRADE | 40% | 0.12% |

See the [Tier System](/token/tier-system) for full tier details and status.

## Gas Fees (Solana Network Fee)

All transactions require a small amount of SOL to cover network gas.

- Typical prediction transaction cost: **~0.001 SOL**
- Recommended minimum wallet balance: **0.05 SOL**
- Gas is separate from trading fees
- Required for order placement, modification, and settlement

Keeping sufficient SOL ensures uninterrupted trading activity.
