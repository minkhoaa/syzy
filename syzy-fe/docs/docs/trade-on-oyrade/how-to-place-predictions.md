## Step-by-Step Walkthrough

### 1. Market Header

At the top of the Order Ticket, you will see the market you are about to trade on:

- **Category** — The market type (e.g., CRYPTO, Politics, Sports).
- **Time Left** — How much time remains before the market expires (e.g., "2D LEFT").
- **Market Question** — The specific condition being predicted (e.g., "Can $WhiteWhale market cap be over $87M at 20 Feb, 2026?").

### 2. Buy or Sell

Choose the action you want to take:

- **Buy** — Open a new position. Select this when you want to enter the market.
- **Sell** — Close or reduce an existing position. Select this when you already hold shares and want to exit.

### 3. Privacy Mode

Below the Buy/Sell toggle, you will see the **Privacy Mode** switch.

- When **off**, your prediction is processed as a standard public transaction.
- When **on**, your trade is routed through Oyrade's Shielded Trading flow — your trade history and position details are encrypted and unlinkable to your wallet.

You can toggle this before placing any order. See [Shielded Trading](/privacy/shielded-predictions) for details.

### 4. Select Outcome: Yes or No

Choose the direction of your prediction:

- **Yes** — You believe the condition will be met (e.g., market cap will be over $87M).
- **No** — You believe the condition will NOT be met.

The percentage displayed next to each outcome (e.g., **62.61%** for Yes, **37.39%** for No) represents the **current implied probability** based on market activity. A higher percentage means the market currently believes that outcome is more likely.

### 5. Enter Amount

Enter how much you want to spend on this prediction:

- **Amount** — The capital you are allocating, denominated in **SOL**.
- **Balance** — Your current available wallet balance is displayed (e.g., "0.0000 SOL").
- **MAX** — Click this to automatically fill the maximum amount you can trade.

### 6. Review Execution Details

Before confirming, review the breakdown at the bottom of the ticket:

- **Fee Tier** — Your current fee tier based on $OYRADE holdings. Displayed as "None" (0.2%) if you do not hold enough tokens for a discount. See [Fees](/trade-on-oyrade/fees) for tier details.
- **Amount After Fee** — The actual amount that will be used to purchase shares after the trading fee is deducted. For example, with a 0.2% fee on 1 SOL, the amount after fee is **0.9980 SOL**.
- **Payout if Win** — The total amount you will receive if your selected outcome resolves correctly (e.g., **1.5973 SOL**).
- **Profit Potential** — Your projected return on investment, shown as a percentage (e.g., **+60%**).

### 7. Confirm Your Prediction

Once you have reviewed all details, click the **Confirm Buy (YES)** or **Confirm Buy (NO)** button to submit your order.

- Your connected wallet will prompt you to approve the transaction.
- Once confirmed on-chain, your position becomes active and visible in your [Portfolio](/trade-on-oyrade/tracking-positions).
