# Managing Positions

Once your order is confirmed, your position becomes active and can be monitored or adjusted at any time before market expiry. Oyrade provides a dedicated portfolio interface where you can track performance, manage exposure, and review past activity.

You can manage your positions by clicking the **Portfolio Page** in the navigation bar on the left side of your screen.

## Portfolio Page

The Portfolio Page gives you a complete overview of your trading activity and account performance.

<figure>
  <img src="/trade-on-oyrade/tracking-positions/portfolio-overview.png" alt="Portfolio Page" />
  <figcaption>Portfolio page showing active positions, balance, and performance metrics.</figcaption>

## Active Positions

This section lists every position that is currently open, showing the key details needed to monitor exposure in real time.

- Outcome selected (Yes/No) and market name
- Shares held and entry price
- Current implied probability / current price
- Current position value and unrealized result
- Action options to reduce, close, or adjust the position

## History

This section stores your completed activity so you can review results and measure performance over time.

- Resolved markets and closed positions
- Entry and exit pricing details
- Realized profit or loss per position
- Final outcome status (win/loss) and settlement record
- Time-based reference for tracking performance trends

## Watchlist

This section keeps markets you are monitoring without entering a position yet, so you can follow movement before deciding to participate.

- Saved markets for quick access
- Live probability and price movement tracking
- Expiry visibility for timing entry decisions
- Simple comparison across multiple markets

## Other Tracking Data

The Portfolio Page also provides summary metrics:

- **Total Balance** - Your overall account value across all assets.
- **Invested** - Total capital currently allocated to open positions.
- **Win Rate** - Percentage of resolved positions that ended in your favor.
- **Asset Allocation** - Breakdown of capital distribution across different markets or positions.

These metrics help you evaluate risk exposure, performance trends, and overall strategy.
