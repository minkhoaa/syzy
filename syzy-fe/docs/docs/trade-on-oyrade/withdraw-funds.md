# Withdraw Funds

Oyrade is a non-custodial platform, which means your funds remain in your own Solana wallet at all times. There is no platform-held balance and no withdrawal requests. When you win a prediction, the payout goes directly to your wallet.

## Claiming Winnings

**Public predictions** are settled automatically when a market resolves. The USDC payout appears in your wallet without any action on your part.

**Shielded predictions** require a manual claim to maintain privacy. To claim your winnings from a shielded position:

1. Go to your [Portfolio](https://oyrade.com/portfolio).
2. Find the resolved shielded position.
3. Click the **"Claim"** button.
4. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

The funds are routed through the privacy pool before arriving in your wallet, which preserves the unlinkability of your prediction.

## Selling Positions Before Resolution

If you do not want to wait for a market to resolve, you can sell your shares at any time at the current market price. Navigate to the market page, click **"Sell"** on your position, and confirm the transaction. The USDC is returned to your wallet immediately.

Keep in mind that selling before resolution means you receive the current market value, which may be higher or lower than the $1.00 payout you would receive if your prediction is correct at settlement.

## Transferring to Other Chains

If you need to move your funds from Solana to another blockchain (Ethereum, Polygon, BNB Chain, etc.), use [Portal Bridge](https://portalbridge.com) to bridge your USDC. Cross-chain transfers typically complete within 15 minutes.

## Converting to Fiat Currency

To cash out to your bank account:

1. Transfer your USDC to a centralized exchange such as Coinbase, Kraken, or Binance.
2. Sell USDC for your local fiat currency (USD, EUR, etc.).
3. Withdraw to your linked bank account.
