# Connect Your Wallet

To use Oyrade, you need a Solana wallet. This is what holds your funds and lets you interact with the platform. If you already have one, you can skip ahead to the connection steps below.

## Setting Up a New Wallet

If you don't have a Solana wallet yet, we recommend [Phantom](https://phantom.app) or [Solflare](https://solflare.com). Both are available as browser extensions and mobile apps.

To set up Phantom:

1. Go to [phantom.app](https://phantom.app) and download the browser extension for your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Brave, or Edge).
2. Open the extension and select **"Create a new wallet"**.
3. Write down your recovery phrase and store it in a safe place. This is the only way to recover your wallet if you lose access to your device.
4. Set a password for everyday use.

## Connecting to Oyrade

Once your wallet is ready:

1. Go to [oyrade.com](https://oyrade.com).
2. Click the **"Connect Wallet"** button in the top-right corner of the page.
3. A modal will appear showing the available wallets detected on your browser. Select yours.
4. Your wallet extension will open a confirmation popup. Click **"Connect"** to approve.

Once connected, your wallet address will appear in the top-right corner of the page, confirming that you are ready to trade.

:::caution
Never share your seed phrase or private keys with anyone. Oyrade will never ask for them. Always double-check that you are on the correct URL (`oyrade.com`) before connecting your wallet.
:::

## Network Requirements

Oyrade runs on **Solana Mainnet**. If your wallet is set to Devnet or another test network, you will need to switch it to **Mainnet Beta** in your wallet's settings and then refresh the page.

## Troubleshooting

If your wallet is not connecting, try the following:

- Make sure the wallet extension is installed and unlocked.
- Refresh the page and try connecting again.
- If you have multiple wallet extensions installed, disable the ones you are not using, as they can sometimes conflict.
- Ensure your wallet is set to Solana Mainnet, not Devnet or another network.

If you are still having trouble, join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/fB6zG5Ck5q) for help.
