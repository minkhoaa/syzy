# About Oyrade

Oyrade is a privacy-first, Solana-native prediction market protocol built for high-velocity information trading. The mission is to become the default venue for traders who require discretion while expressing directional views on events, narratives, and market outcomes.

Prediction markets are entering a scale phase as mainstream platforms demonstrate multi-billion-dollar annual volume and accelerating adoption. The category's growth is driven by three structural forces:

- **Information Financialization**: Social and macro signals move faster than traditional markets; traders increasingly monetize information advantages.
- **Always-On Markets**: Crypto-native participants expect 24/7 access, instant execution, and continuous pricing.
- **Composable On-Chain Infrastructure**: Stable settlement and programmable liquidity enable new market formats and distribution models.

Oyrade targets the segment where on-chain transparency becomes a constraint: alpha-focused participants who require privacy, speed, and capital efficiency within a unified system.
