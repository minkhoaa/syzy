# Faucet (Devnet)

The Devnet Token Faucet lets you claim free OYRADE tokens on Solana devnet to explore and test the Oyrade prediction market platform.

<figure>
  <img src="/token/faucet/faucet-unconnected.png" alt="Devnet Token Faucet" />
  <figcaption>The Devnet Faucet page — connect your wallet to claim free OYRADE tokens.</figcaption>
</figure>

## How to Claim

1. Go to the Devnet Faucet page.
2. Click **Connect Wallet** in the top right corner.
3. Approve the connection in your wallet (make sure your wallet is set to **Solana Devnet**).
4. Once connected, click the **Claim 1,000 OYRADE** button.
5. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

<figure>
  <img src="/token/faucet/faucet-action.png" alt="Claim 1,000 OYRADE" />
  <figcaption>After connecting your wallet, click "Claim 1,000 OYRADE" to receive devnet tokens.</figcaption>
</figure>

## Faucet Rules

- **Devnet Only** — Tokens are issued on Solana devnet and have no real value.
- **1,000 OYRADE / claim** — Each claim gives you exactly 1,000 OYRADE tokens.
- **1h cooldown** — You must wait 1 hour between claims.

Your current devnet OYRADE balance is displayed on the page after connecting your wallet.
