# Staking & Governance

Stake your $OYRADE tokens to earn SOL rewards from platform trading fees. The staking page provides a complete interface for staking, claiming rewards, unstaking, and (coming soon) locking tokens for governance power.

## Staking Overview

<figure>
  <img src="/token/governance/overview.png" alt="Staking Overview" />
  <figcaption>The staking page showing total staked, reward rate, period end date, and reward pool.</figcaption>
</figure>

The staking dashboard displays key metrics at a glance:

- **Total Staked** — The total amount of OYRADE currently staked across all participants.
- **Reward Rate** — The current SOL reward rate distributed to the staking pool per day.
- **Period Ends** — The end date of the current reward period.
- **Reward Pool** — The total SOL available for distribution in the current period.

## How Revenue Is Distributed

Every trade on Oyrade generates a 0.2% fee (0.3% for shielded predictions). This revenue is split into three parts:

| Allocation | Percentage | Purpose |
|------------|-----------|---------|
| Stakers | 30% | Distributed in SOL to users who stake $OYRADE |
| Buyback & Burn | 12% | Used to purchase $OYRADE from the open market and remove it from circulation |
| Operations | 58% | Funds development, infrastructure, and growth initiatives |

## How to Stake

<figure>
  <img src="/token/governance/stake.png" alt="Stake OYRADE" />
  <figcaption>Enter the amount of OYRADE you want to stake and confirm the transaction.</figcaption>
</figure>

1. Go to the **Stake** tab on the staking page.
2. Enter the amount of OYRADE you want to stake, or use the percentage slider (25%, 50%, 75%, 100%) to select a portion of your available balance.
3. Review the **Reward rate** and the amount you will stake.
4. Click **Stake** and confirm the transaction in your wallet.

You begin earning SOL rewards immediately after your stake is confirmed.

## Claim Rewards

<figure>
  <img src="/token/governance/claim.png" alt="Claim Rewards" />
  <figcaption>View your pending SOL rewards and claim them to your wallet.</figcaption>
</figure>

Navigate to the **Claim** tab to collect your accumulated SOL rewards:

- **Pending SOL Rewards** — The amount of SOL you have earned and can claim now.
- **Reward Rate** — The current daily distribution rate for the entire pool.
- **Your Pool Share** — Your percentage of the total staking pool, which determines your share of rewards.
- **Period Ends** — When the current reward period concludes.

Click **Claim** to send your pending SOL rewards directly to your wallet.

## Unstake

<figure>
  <img src="/token/governance/unstake.png" alt="Unstake OYRADE" />
  <figcaption>Withdraw your staked OYRADE tokens. Pending SOL rewards will also be claimed automatically.</figcaption>
</figure>

To withdraw your staked tokens, go to the **Unstake** tab:

1. Enter the amount of OYRADE you want to unstake, or use the percentage slider.
2. Review **Remaining staked** and **You will receive**.
3. Click **Unstake** and confirm the transaction.

When you unstake, any pending SOL rewards are automatically claimed and sent to your wallet along with your OYRADE tokens.

## veOYRADE Locking (Coming Soon)

<figure>
  <img src="/token/governance/lock-soon.png" alt="veOYRADE Locking" />
  <figcaption>veOYRADE locking — lock your tokens for 1–4 years to unlock governance power and boosted rewards.</figcaption>
</figure>

The **Lock** tab will allow you to lock your OYRADE tokens for a period of 1 to 4 years in exchange for **veOYRADE** — a non-transferable token that grants:

- **Governance voting power** over protocol decisions (market creation, fee changes, treasury spending, upgrades).
- **Boosted staking rewards** up to 2.5x compared to standard staking.

This feature is currently under development and will be activated in a future update.
