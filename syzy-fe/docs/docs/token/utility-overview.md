# $OYRADE Token

$OYRADE is the native token of the Oyrade protocol. It serves multiple functions within the ecosystem, from reducing your trading costs to earning a share of protocol revenue and participating in governance decisions.

## Token Details

The total supply of $OYRADE is **1,000,000,000 tokens**, issued as an SPL token on Solana. The token was launched via a fair launch on Pump.fun.

## Core Utilities

**Fee Discounts** - Simply holding $OYRADE in your wallet reduces your trading fees. No staking or locking is required. Holding 10,000+ tokens gives you a 20% discount, 100,000+ gives you 40%, and 1,000,000+ gives you the maximum 60% discount. See [Fees](/trade-on-oyrade/fees) for details on how this affects your trading costs.

**Revenue Share** - Staking $OYRADE entitles you to a share of the protocol's trading fee revenue. 30% of all fees collected are distributed weekly in USDC to stakers. The longer you lock your stake, the larger your share of the reward pool. See [Staking & Governance](/token/governance) for the full details.

**Governance** - Locking $OYRADE for a period of time converts it into veOYRADE (vote-escrowed OYRADE), a non-transferable token that gives you voting power over protocol decisions. veOYRADE holders can vote on new market creation, fee structure changes, treasury spending, and protocol upgrades. See [Governance](/token/governance) for more.

**Privacy Tiers** - Higher token holdings unlock enhanced privacy features, including multi-hop mixing, dedicated privacy infrastructure, and MEV protection. See the [Tier System](/token/tier-system) for the full breakdown of benefits at each level.

## Design Philosophy

The $OYRADE token model is inspired by the vote-escrow (ve(3,3)) approach used in protocols like Curve and Velodrome. The core idea is to reward long-term commitment: the longer you hold, stake, or lock your tokens, the greater your benefits. Protocol revenue flows directly to stakers, which aligns token holder incentives with the growth and success of the platform. Governance power is concentrated in the hands of those who have demonstrated the most long-term commitment through locking.
