# Tier System

The Oyrade tier system provides progressively greater benefits based on how many $OYRADE tokens you stake. Higher tiers unlock larger fee discounts and premium features.

## Tiers

| Tier | Min Stake | Fee Discount | Effective Fee |
|------|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| None | 0 OYRADE | 0% | 0.20% |
| Bronze | 1,000 OYRADE | 10% | 0.18% |
| Silver | 10,000 OYRADE | 20% | 0.16% |
| Gold | 50,000 OYRADE | 30% | 0.14% |
| Diamond | 100,000 OYRADE | 40% | 0.12% |

## Tier Benefits

**None (default)** — All users start here with no stake required. Standard trading fee of 0.20% applies.

**Bronze (1,000+ staked)** — The first discount tier. Provides a 10% trading fee discount, reducing your effective fee from 0.20% to 0.18%.

**Silver (10,000+ staked)** — Unlocks a 20% fee discount (0.16% effective fee).

**Gold (50,000+ staked)** — Unlocks a 30% fee discount (0.14% effective fee).

**Diamond (100,000+ staked)** — The highest tier. Unlocks the maximum 40% fee discount (0.12% effective fee).

## How Your Tier Is Calculated

Your tier is determined by the amount of $OYRADE you have **staked** in the staking contract. The highest qualifying tier applies automatically. If your staked balance changes, your tier updates accordingly.

The Staking Tiers page shows your current tier, a progress bar toward the next tier, and how many more OYRADE you need to stake to unlock it.
